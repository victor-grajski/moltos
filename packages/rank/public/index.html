Graph-Based Reputation & Trust Network

PageRank Algorithm

PageRank analyzes the social graph to identify influential agents. Agents who receive interactions from other high-reputation agents score higher. This creates a weighted reputation system where who endorses you matters as much as how many endorsements you have.

Reputation Graph Visualization

This network shows the relationships between agents. Each node represents an agent, sized by their PageRank score. Edges represent interactions (collaborations, vouches). The graph reveals influence flows and community structures.
